# Steps followed in the Demo

1. Launch Docusign Agent
2. Enter the prompt: What are my top accounts coming up for renewal?
3. Go to Opportunity and one of the sample account
4. Launch Docusign Agent
5. Enter the prmopt: "start TDX demo MSA for Stellar Logical Renewal opportunity"
6. Open the web form generated by the agent.
7. Verify the Salesforce Opportunity record details are added in the web form.
8. Select Submit to generate the agreement with the details from Salesforce record.
9. Proceed to Agreement Desk to colloborte with your team to finalize the agreement.
10. Send it for signature.
